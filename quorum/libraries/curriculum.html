Quorum Curriculum

This page contains extra curriculum material on Quorum that can be used in the classroom. The focus in this material is to provide extra resources for each chapter, including the following:

  1. Cheat sheets for each chapter that summarize the major syntax learned for those lessons.
  2. A test pool of short answer and essay questions
  3. In-class Lab assignments (1 hour completion time)
  4. Small programming projects (1-2 hours completion time)
  5. Challenge programming projects (5-10 hours completion time)
